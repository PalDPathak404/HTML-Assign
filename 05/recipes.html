Panner Makhani



Paneer Makhani is a classic North Indian curry featuring soft paneer cubes in a velvety tomato and butter sauce. The gravy gets its rich, creamy texture from cashews and cream, creating a luxurious mouthfeel. With a perfect balance of tangy, sweet, and mildly spiced flavors, it stands out as an ultimate vegetarian comfort food.



Ingredients :-

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter
  • A pinch of turmeric and red chili powder (optional, for searing)
  • Cashews & Ginger-Garlic Pastte
  • Honey or Sugar
  • Tomatoes & pureed


Cooking Steps :-

  1. Prepare the Paneer
  2. Make the Cashew Paste
  3. Start the Gravy
  4. Add the ginger-garlic paste
  5. Pour the paste and Stir in the cashew paste
  6. Add about 1/2 water to the gray paste formwed there.

Chole Masala



A spicy, tangy Punjabi dish prepared with chickpeas (chole) cooked in an onion-tomato base with aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. It has a bold flavor and is often served with bhature or rice.



Ingredients :-

  • Dried chickpeas
  • Water
  • Other Masals luike Cardamom , Chilli(black) etc.
  • Urad dal & Fenugreek seeds
  • Oil or ghee


Cooking Steps :-

  1. Prepare the Batter
  2. Drain the dal and grind it
  3. Drain the rice and grind it
  4. Combine both pastes in a large bowl, mix well
  5. Once fermented, add salt
  6. Add the turmeric powder

Dal Tadka



A classic lentil dish where yellow lentils (like toor or moong dal) are cooked until soft, then finished with a tempering (tadka) of ghee, cumin seeds, garlic, onions, and red chili. It has a comforting, smoky flavor and is typically eaten with rice or roti.



Ingredients :-

  • Toor dal
  • Water
  • Turmeric powder & Salt
  • Ghee and Cumin seeds
  • Ginger-garlic paste
