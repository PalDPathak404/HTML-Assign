National News



  1. India-US Trade Talks in New Delhi

    India and the United States are scheduled to hold trade negotiations in New Delhi. These come shortly after the U.S. imposed steep tariffs on Indian exports (some goods now taxed 25%, pushing total duties to ~50%) in response to India’s continued purchases of Russian oil.

  2. Supreme Court & Bihar SIR Warning

    The Supreme Court has warned that the voter list revision under Bihar’s Special Investigation Report (SIR) could be struck down if found illegal. This ruling will have implications beyond Bihar and could set a pan-India precedent.

  3. India-Mauritius Currency Trade Deal

    India and Mauritius will begin trading in their respective national currencies, reducing dependence on third-party currencies like the U.S. dollar. A special economic package of about US$680 million was also announced to support key sectors in Mauritius.

  4. Above-Normal Rainfall Forecast

    The India Meteorological Department (IMD) has forecast that September is likely to be wetter than average. Heavy rainfall warnings have been issued for regions like Uttarakhand, north Rajasthan, south Haryana, Delhi, and parts of north Chhattisgarh.

  5. India Participates in Russia-Belarus Military Drills

    India has sent 65 troops from its Kumaon Regiment to participate in the Russian-led Zapad military exercises in western Russia. The drills involve 30,000 Russian and Belarusian soldiers and have raised concerns among NATO allies.
