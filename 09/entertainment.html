Entertainment News



  1. Priya Sachdev Makes First Public Appearance Amid Legal Battle

    Priya Sachdev Kapur has made her first public appearance since the death of her husband, Sunjay Kapur. This comes amid an ongoing legal dispute over his estate, which includes a significant inheritance claim involving Karisma Kapoor's children. The situation has garnered widespread media attention.

  2. Ibrahim Ali Khan Embraces Authenticity in New Ad

    Ibrahim Ali Khan has chosen to dub his real voice, which includes a slight lisp, for a new advertisement. He shared that he was "born imperfect" and believes that "what's real is far more powerful than what's perfect," highlighting his commitment to authenticity.

  3. Keerthy Suresh Discusses Bollywood Transition

    Actress Keerthy Suresh, known for her role in Mahanati, has opened up about her transition from South Indian cinema to Bollywood. She expressed her desire to experiment with diverse roles and emphasized the importance of getting her Bollywood debut "right."

  4. Anupam Kher Rejects Lifetime Achievement Awards

    Veteran actor Anupam Kher has revealed that he declines lifetime achievement awards, stating that he is not content with his legacy and continues to seek challenging roles. He also discussed what Hollywood can learn from Bollywood's approach to filmmaking.

  5. Divya Khosla Kumar Critiques Cosmetic Surgery Trends

    Filmmaker and actress Divya Khosla Kumar has criticized the increasing trend of cosmetic surgery among celebrities, including international figures like Kylie Jenner. She expressed concern that many faces now look "pretty much the same" and advocated for embracing natural beauty.
